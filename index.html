Our Services

OTG Trucking provides comprehensive logistics solutions across Zimbabwe and neighboring countries. We specialize in both long-haul and local freight transportation, offering reliable cargo delivery services for businesses of all sizes. Our modern fleet and experienced team ensure safe, timely, and cost-effective transportation of goods, while our real-time tracking systems provide peace of mind throughout the delivery process. Whether you need regular shipments or one-time transportation solutions, we're here to serve your logistics needs.
